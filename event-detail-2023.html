Overview

SSTH isn’t merely an event—it’s a voyage of self-discovery, an odyssey where innovation unfurls its wings, and limitless possibilities dance in the spotlight. This hallowed platform has consistently nurtured the seeds of future scientists, engineers, and thought leaders, cultivating a fertile ground for brilliance to take root. Our unwavering commitment to fostering curiosity and critical thinking remains the compass guiding this transformative journey.

Be With Engineering:

Picture an arena where passion and purpose intertwine, where collaboration ignites the forge of groundbreaking innovation. Here, your ideas aren’t mere sparks; they’re cosmic catalysts, shaping the very fabric of engineering’s future. SSTH transcends the mundane competition model—it’s an invitation to plunge into the ocean of engineering excellence, to co-create visionary projects that ripple across reality. In this unparalleled odyssey, SSTH beckons engineering enthusiasts to harness their skills, grapple with cutting-edge challenges, and emerge as trailblazers, leaving stardust in their wake.

The Grand Overture

SSTH 2023—a symphony of intellect and ingenuity—resonated across dimensions. Whether you’re a budding scientist, a tech virtuoso, or a muse-chaser, SSTH unfurls its canvas for you. The challenges spanned the spectrum: from quantum leaps in scientific experiments to elegant solutions woven from the threads of technology, and even the alchemy of creative problem-solving. As you navigated this celestial labyrinth, SSTH didn’t merely test your knowledge; it whispered secrets to your soul, urging you to ascend.

Distinguished Voices:

Five luminaries graced our cosmic stage: Chief Guest Vikas Gadi ,Guest of Honour Mr Murali Manohar, Chairman Mr Manjunath Bhandary, Director Dr Manjappa Sarathi and Principal Dr S .S Injaganeri ,Their presence wasn’t just applause it was a meteor shower of wisdom, igniting constellations of inspiration. SSTH, bathed in their brilliance, became a celestial convergence—an event etched in stardust, where dreams collided and birthed galaxies.

And as the cosmic curtain fell, SSTH crowned its champions—the architects of tomorrow, their names etched in quarks and quasars.
