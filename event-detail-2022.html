Overview

SSTH is not just an event, it's a journey of self-discovery, innovation, and limitless possibilities. Designed to unleash the potential of young minds, this platform has consistently proven to be a breeding ground for future scientists, engineers, and thought leaders. Our commitment to fostering a culture of curiosity and critical thinking remains unwavering. The SSTH 2022 event unfolded as a symphony of intellect and innovation, leaving an indelible mark with its grand success, a testament to the brilliance that converged under its banner.

Be With Engineering:

Imagine a dynamic arena where passion converges with purpose, where collaboration becomes the catalyst for groundbreaking innovation, and your ideas possess the transformative potential to shape the future of engineering. SSTH transcends the conventional competition model; it stands as an unparalleled opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of engineering excellence, collaborate on visionary projects, and pave the way for real-world impact. In this unparalleled journey, SSTH beckons engineering enthusiasts to harness their skills, tackle cutting-edge challenges, and emerge as trailblazers in the field.

Grand Finale of SSTH:

SSTH 2022 was a massive success. Whether you're a budding scientist, a tech enthusiast, or a creative thinker, SSTH provides a platform to showcase your talents and refine your skills. The challenges are diverse, ranging from cutting-edge scientific experiments to innovative technological solutions and creative problem-solving. Your journey through SSTH will not only test your knowledge but also elevate your potential to new heights.

Distinguished by their expertise, our five illustrious guests, Sushil Mungekar, Prithvi Sai Penumadu, Rajasree Devineni, Dr. B E Rangaswamy and Mukesh S brought a wealth of knowledge, inspiring participants with insights that transcended disciplines for the event. Their presence added a touch of excellence, turning SSTH into an unforgettable convergence of brilliance and experience. The event was concluded with an award ceremony for the prize winners.
